The cow

A story of a cow

In the emerald valley of Cloverbrook, nestled against a backdrop of snow-capped mountains, lived a young cow named Willow. Unlike her sisters, content with their days of munching on wildflowers and basking in the sun, Willow dreamt of adventure. Tales of explorers mapping uncharted lands filled her udder with a wanderlust she couldn't suppress.One crisp morning, as the mist swirled around the dew-kissed meadows, Willow made her move. Slinking past the snoring farmer, she trotted past the slumbering herd, her heart drumming a staccato rhythm against her ribs. The world beyond the fence beckoned, whispering promises of sun-drenched valleys and hidden streams.
